Frequently Asked Questions

Is InterGen safe?

Yes, we verify all users and facilitate secure matches based on preferences and compatibility.

How are matches made?

Our algorithm and team consider location, support needs, interests, and personality to create the best matches.

Can I leave the program anytime?

Yes, both students and seniors can end participation with appropriate notice as outlined in our terms.

What support do students provide?

Students may help with light chores, errands, tech help, or simply providing companionship.
